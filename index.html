Game Help

Welcome to the game!

Here are some controls and tips:

  • [ W ]: Move Up
  • [ A ]: Move Left
  • [ S ]: Move Down
  • [ D ]: Move Right
  • Click on the grid to interact with the environment.
  • Check your inventory by clicking on [ Inventory ].
  • Keep an eye on your health bar!
  • Optional: Use a .bake text file to import your previous, saved world.
